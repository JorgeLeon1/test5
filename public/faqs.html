Frequently Asked Questions

How should I use the location visualization on the allocation page?

Tip: Visualizing a location within the warehouse can often provide valuable context when determining whether it is the most appropriate spot for allocation.

Recommendation: Review the visual layout of locations—this may be easiest using the downloaded version of the data. Consider how you would allocate items manually, then compare this with the algorithm’s allocation.

If there are differences, try to infer the reason behind them. This often helps clarify whether the system logic aligns with your intended strategy, and where adjustments may be necessary.

If you have any questions or require further clarification, please don't hesitate to contact us via the Help menu.
